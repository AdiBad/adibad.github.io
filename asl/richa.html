Welcome to my webpage!

Hi!

This is Richa here, a school teacher, a gardener, a mother (and that takes care of all other professions in my repertoire)! I have two children. I am a post graduate in English and my hobbies include reading, cooking and yes gardening-that surprisingly relaxes me with the manual effort it demands of me.
I am fond of everything God has created and find imperfections perfectly perfect! :) I have now graduated myself from my other roles to become a full fledged writer.
I've debuted with a short poetry book called 'The Vagrant', that will greatly appeal the younger lot, as well as school teachers though credibly, it has seen appreciation from other quarters of professions too. My second book 'A Slick Life' is a treasured memoir of an interesting oilman whose life saw the many changes happening in India and its people. It makes for a good read as it pulls you in as a part of its own, a feeling better felt by reading than simply told.
