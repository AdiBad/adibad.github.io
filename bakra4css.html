Welcome to my webpage!

Hi!

This is Richa here, a school teacher, a gardener, a mother (and that takes care of all other professions in my repertoire)! I have two children. I am a post graduate in English and my hobbies include reading, cooking and yes gardening that surprisingly relaxes me with the manual effort that it demands of me. I am fond of everything God has created and find imperfections perfectly perfect! :) I have now graduated myself from my other roles to become many other things by becoming a full fledged writer.
I've debuted with a short poetry book called 'The Vagrant', that will greatly appeal the younger lot as well as school teachers although it has seen appreciation from other quarters as well. My second book 'A Slick Life' is an interesting memoir of an interesting oilman whose life saw the many changes happening in India and its people. It makes for a good read as well as make you a part of its own as you'll proceed.
